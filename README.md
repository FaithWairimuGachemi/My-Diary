# My Diary App

My Diary is a simple and private journaling application for Android. It allows you to quickly write down your thoughts, experiences, and memories, keeping them safe and organized on your device.

## Features

*   **Google Sign-In**: Securely sign in with your Google account.
*   **Add New Entries**: Easily create new diary entries with a title and content. The app automatically saves the current date for each entry.
*   **View All Entries**: See a list of all your diary entries on the main screen, with the most recent entry appearing at the top.
*   **View a Specific Entry**: Tap on any entry in the list to view its full content in a read-only mode.
*   **Edit and Delete**: Each entry has a three-dot menu with options to:
    *   **Edit**: Modify the title or content of an existing entry.
    *   **Delete**: Permanently remove an entry.
*   **Data Persistence**: All your diary entries are saved locally on your device, so they are available every time you open the app.

## How to Use

1.  **Sign In**: Open the app and sign in using your Google account.
2.  **Add an Entry**:
    *   Tap the floating "add" button at the bottom right of the main screen.
    *   Enter a title and your thoughts for the day.
    *   Tap the "Save" button.
3.  **Manage Entries**:
    *   On the main screen, find the entry you want to manage.
    *   Tap the three dots on the right side of the entry.
    *   Choose "View," "Edit," or "Delete" from the menu.

## Technologies Used

*   Java
*   Android SDK
*   Google Sign-In for Android
*   AndroidX Libraries (AppCompat, RecyclerView, etc.)
*   Gson for data serialization
